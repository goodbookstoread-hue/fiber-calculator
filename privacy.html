Fiber Tracker Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 23, 2026

Magarya Waitara, MD (“we,” “us,” or “our”) operates Fiber Tracker (the “App” or “Service”). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By creating an account or using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • A password (stored in encrypted form; we never see or store your password in plain text)

1.2 Food and Fiber Data

When you use the App's core tracking features, we collect and store:

  • Descriptions of foods and meals you enter
  • Estimated fiber content calculated from those entries
  • Your personal daily fiber goal
  • Timestamps of when entries were logged

1.3 Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect precise location data, payment card numbers (these are handled directly by our payment processor, if and when you subscribe to a paid plan), or any health information beyond what you voluntarily type into the food-logging feature.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's core functionality, including calculating fiber estimates and displaying your history
  • Personalize your experience, such as displaying your name and tracking progress toward your individual fiber goal
  • Generate food suggestions intended to help you reach your daily fiber goal
  • Communicate with you about your account, such as confirming your email address
  • Improve and maintain the reliability of the Service

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. How Your Information Is Processed and Stored

To provide the App's features, certain information is shared with the following categories of service providers, solely for the purpose of operating the Service:

  • Database and authentication hosting providers, who store your account and food-log data securely
  • Artificial intelligence processing providers, who receive the food descriptions you enter in order to return fiber estimates and suggestions. These providers process this data to generate a response and do not use it to identify you personally.
  • Web hosting and infrastructure providers, who host the application itself

These providers are contractually and technically restricted to using your data only to deliver the requested functionality.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your account information and food-log history for as long as your account remains active, so that you can view your historical data at any time.

Your right to delete your data. You may request deletion of your account and all associated personal data at any time by emailing us at fiberforhealthnow@gmail.com. Upon a verified request, we will delete your account information and food logs from our active systems within 30 days, except where retention is required to comply with a legal obligation.

5. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including the ability to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information (you can update most information directly within the App)
  • Request deletion of your account and data, as described in Section 4
  • Withdraw consent to data processing by closing your account

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at fiberforhealthnow@gmail.com.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the relevant minimum age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

7. Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards, including encrypted password storage and access-controlled databases, to protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you by posting the updated policy within the App or by emailing the address associated with your account.
